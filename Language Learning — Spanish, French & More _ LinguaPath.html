Language Learning for Every Level

LinguaPath — Learn a New Language One Conversation at a Time

LinguaPath is a free language learning platform combining short audio-first lessons, native speaker dialogues, and writing practice for learners from absolute beginner through advanced fluency.

Languages Offered

Active courses include Spanish (Latin American and Castilian), French, German, Italian, Brazilian Portuguese, Mandarin Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Arabic (Modern Standard), Hindi, Dutch, Swedish, and English as a Second Language. Courses for Vietnamese, Turkish, and Greek are in beta.

Proficiency Levels

Lessons are mapped to the Common European Framework of Reference, covering A1 and A2 beginner, B1 and B2 intermediate, and C1 advanced bands. A short placement test routes returning learners to the right starting point.

Test Prep

Targeted preparation tracks support the DELE and SIELE for Spanish, DELF and DALF for French, Goethe-Zertifikat for German, CILS for Italian, HSK for Mandarin, JLPT for Japanese, TOPIK for Korean, TOEFL and IELTS for English, and AP language exams.

How Lessons Work

Each ten-minute lesson opens with a contextual dialogue, introduces ten new words, drills pronunciation through speech recognition, and ends with a short written or spoken exercise. Streak tracking and spaced repetition keep vocabulary in long-term memory.

Conversation Practice

Live small-group conversation rooms run daily across time zones, organized by language and level. Learners can also book one-on-one tutor sessions and exchange voice messages with study partners around the world.
